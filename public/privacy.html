Privacy Policy

Last updated: June 2025

stado ("we", "us", "our") is committed to protecting your privacy. This policy explains what data we collect, how we use it, and your rights.

What we collect

When you sign in with Google, we receive your name, email address, and profile photo from your Google account. We store this to create your stado profile.

When you post or join games, we store the game details and your participation. This includes your display name appearing in the players list for games you join.

We do not sell your data to third parties.

How we use your data

Your name and photo are displayed to other players on games you join or host. Your email is only used to identify your account and is never shown to other users.

No-show counts are stored on your profile and may be visible to hosts when you attempt to join their game.

Data storage

Your data is stored in Google Firebase (Firestore), which is hosted in the United States. By using stado, you consent to your data being processed there.

Deleting your data

You can request deletion of your account and all associated data by emailing support@stado.app. We will action this within 30 days.

Contact

Questions? Email support@stado.app.
